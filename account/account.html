ABOUT US

Shining a light on the Pinoy BAYANIHAN SPIRIT

We're big believers of bayanihan—the Filipino custom of people working together to help a neighbor, lessening the workload and making things easier. At Pinoy Luminaries, we want to make it possible for OFWs to help Filipinos back home through quick and easy loans.

Pinoy Luminaries is a fintech start-up in the Philippines committed to improving the lives of overseas Filipino workers (OFWs) through digital credit.

Our pioneering fintech platform facilitates loans from OFWs willing to extend a helping hand to their fellow Pinoys, whether to help them travel for a job abroad or to assist their family members back home.

This is the first fintech platform powered for and by OFWs.
